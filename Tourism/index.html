What Does Ghana Taste Like?

Sense

The vibrant hues of garden eggs in our Kontomire Stew, the golden glow of perfectly fried plantains, and the inviting steam rising from a bowl of spicy Light Soup."

Smell

Be drawn in by the earthy aroma of roasting groundnuts, the pungent allure of fermented corn dough for Kenkey, and the sweet, tangy notes of ripe mango."

Taste

Experience the fiery kick of Shito, the comforting starchiness of Fufu, the creamy richness of Groundnut Soup, and the refreshing burst of local fruit juices like Asaana."
